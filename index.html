Help & Documentation

Getting Started

Welcome to the Amputee Football Player Registry System (AFPRS). This system helps manage players, training programs, medical records, equipment, and more for amputee football teams.

To get started:

  • Use the navigation tabs at the top to access different sections
  • Add players to your roster in the Players section
  • Create training programs in the Training section
  • Track medical records in the Medical section

Player Management

The Players section allows you to:

  • Add new players with detailed information including amputation type
  • Edit existing player records
  • Filter players by status (active, injured)
  • Export and import player data

Training Programs

The Training section includes:

  • Specialized exercises adapted for amputee football players
  • Ability to create custom exercises
  • Assign exercises to individual players
  • Track completion of training programs

Medical Records

The Medical section helps you:

  • Record injuries and treatments
  • Track rehabilitation progress
  • Set follow-up reminders
  • Generate medical reports

Equipment Management

Manage team equipment through:

  • Crutch Management for tracking player crutches
  • Inventory system for uniforms and equipment
  • Size distribution tracking
  • Maintenance records
